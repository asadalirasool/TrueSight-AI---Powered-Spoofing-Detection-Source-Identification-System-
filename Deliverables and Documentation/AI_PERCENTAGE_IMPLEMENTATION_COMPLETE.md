## 📊 Model Architecture

```
Input Video (40 frames)
        ↓
    Frame Extraction
        ↓
    Preprocessing (DeiT processor)
        ↓
    DeiT Backbone (facebook/deit-base-patch16-224)
        ↓
    Feature Vector (768-dim)
        ↓
    ┌─────────────┬──────────────┬─────────────────┐
    ↓             ↓              ↓                 ↓
Percentage    Artifact       Tool            Confidence
Head          Head           Head            Score
(MSE Loss)    (BCE Loss)     (CrossEntropy)  (Derived)
    ↓             ↓              ↓                 ↓
AI % (0-100)  5 Artifact    7 Tool          Overall
              Scores        Classes         Confidence
```
